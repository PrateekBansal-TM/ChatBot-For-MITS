About MITS Gwalior

Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) is a premier engineering college located in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, India. Established in 1957, it is one of the oldest engineering colleges in the state. The college is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

MITS Gwalior offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various branches of engineering, including Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology, and Chemical Engineering. The college also offers a Master of Business Administration (MBA) program.

Admission Process at MITS Gwalior

Admission to undergraduate courses at MITS Gwalior is based on the candidate's performance in the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). For admission to postgraduate courses, candidates must appear for the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) or the Management Aptitude Test (MAT) for the MBA program.

Eligibility Criteria

For Undergraduate Courses

To be eligible for undergraduate courses at MITS Gwalior, candidates must have passed 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. Candidates must also have a valid JEE score.
